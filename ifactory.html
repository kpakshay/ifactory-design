LONG SCROLL : THE GOOD STUFF
VISUAL CREATIVITY

There is more visual freedom in parallel website design. Rather than focusing on block style information architecture,you can create refreshing and engaging viduals such as animations or beautiful photography, that acts as a continum, ultimately inspiring users to scroll further down the page
